Early life and Education

  • 1) Sridevi was born at Meenampatti village near Sivakasi of present-day Tamil Nadu, India on 13 August 1963 to Ayyapan and Rajeswari.
  • 2) Sridevi started her career as a child actor at the age of four in the Tamil movie Kandhan Karunai in 1967. Subsequently, she played the role of young Muruga in Thunaivan.
  • 3) She did her schooling education at Velankanni Matriculation School and did her college at Anna Adarsh, Anna University, Madras University.

Leading Roles and Success

  • 1) Sridevi landed her first leading role in the Tamil film Moondru Mudichu directed by K. Balachander.[35] She followed it with a number of films with Kamal Haasan and Rajinikanth.
  • 2) In 1976, Sridevi landed her first leading role in the Tamil film Moondru Mudichu directed by K. Balachander.[35] She followed it with a number of films with Kamal Haasan and Rajinikanth.
  • 3) Sridevi made her debut as a heroine in Hindi films in Solva Sawan in 1979.[47] 4 years later, she was signed to star opposite Jeetendra in Himmatwala (remake of Telugu film Ooruki Monagadu).

Stardom and Hiatus

  • 1) Sridevi followed the success of Nagina by playing a goofy crime journalist in the science fiction film Mr. India (1987) opposite Anil Kapoor, which was described by Rediff as "one of the most iconic films of its time".
  • 2) Sridevi's imitation of Charlie Chaplin in the film was described by The Times of India as "the most hilarious act she has ever done".
  • 3) Yash Chopra's romantic musical saga Chandni, with Sridevi playing the title role. The film emerged as one of the biggest blockbusters of 1989.Cited by The Times of India as "one of the most watched films of Indian cinema".

Personal Life

  • 1) In 1996, she married producer Boney Kapoor. The couple had two daughters, Janhvi, who is herself an actress, and Khushi.
  • 2) Despite being known for her portrayals of a strong, vivacious and exuberant woman on-screen, Sridevi was an extremely introverted and reserved person off-screen.
  • 3) Having begun to act when she was just 4, she did not complete formal education or get much of a chance to interact with kids of her own age..

Death and Funeral

  • 1) Sridevi was pronounced dead on 24 February 2018 at 9:00 in her hotel room where her husband had found her.
  • 2) On 3 March, Sridevi's ashes were flown into Tamil Nadu via Chennai by her husband and her two daughters Jhanvi Kapoor and Khushi Kapoor and later taken to and immersed in the sea off the coast of Rameswaram.
